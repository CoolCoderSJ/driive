About Us

DRIIVE is a non-profit organization committed to reducing the digital divide and promoting digital equity. Our organization recognizes the transformative power of technology and believes that access to digital resources should be available to everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances.

In today's digital age, being connected is no longer a luxury but a necessity. Unfortunately, many individuals and communities face barriers that prevent them from accessing and harnessing the full potential of digital technologies. We aim to address these disparities and create a more inclusive digital landscape.

At DRIIVE, we work tirelessly to bridge the digital divide by providing essential resources, education, and support. We collaborate with local communities, educational institutions, businesses, and government agencies to develop and implement effective strategies that empower individuals to navigate the digital world with confidence.

Our Core Focus Areas

Access to Technology

We strive to ensure that individuals have access to affordable digital devices and reliable internet connectivity. By partnering with technology companies and organizations, we facilitate the distribution of refurbished devices and support the establishment of community centers equipped with computer labs and internet access.

Digital Literacy

According to us, mastering digital literacy is the key to utilizing technology's advantages. In order to equip people with the knowledge and abilities to use technology efficiently, traverse the internet safely, and make use of digital resources for both personal and professional advancement, our organization offers complete digital literacy programs and training sessions.

Community Engagement

We actively interact with the surrounding areas, establishing trust and gaining insight into the special requirements and difficulties that each community faces. We promote a sense of digital empowerment, collaboration, and digital inclusion as a shared duty through neighborhood-based projects, workshops, and community gatherings.

Advocacy and Policy

We support measures that support accessible internet access, reduce the digital divide, and give priority to digital inclusion. Working with legislators, stakeholders, and advocacy organizations, we aim to create an environment that supports equal digital opportunities for all.
